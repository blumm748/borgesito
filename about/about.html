Jorge Luis Borges

Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

The site features the author's most notable works, alongside various interactive features that enhance the overall experience.

His early works might seem baroque, as if he longs for an old world now out of reach. After many years he attains a secret and modest complexity. It is within his allegories and metaphors where we encounter a terrifying truth:

Time is a river which sweeps me along, but I am the river; it is a tiger which destroys me, but I am the tiger; it is a fire which consumes me, but I am the fire. The world, unfortunately, is real;

I, unfortunately, am Borges.
